Flashcards





AUTOTROPHIC NUTRITION

Nutrition in which organisms make their own food using raw materials. Organisms who make their own food are called autotrophs.

HETEROTROPHIC NUTRITION

Nutrition in which organisms depends upon other organisms for their food. The organisms which depend upon other organisms are called heterotrophs.

PHOTOSYNTHESIS

The process by which autotrophs make their own food using simple substansces such as sunlight, water, etc. is called photosynthesis.
